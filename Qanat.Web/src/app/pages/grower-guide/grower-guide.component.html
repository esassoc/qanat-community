Growers, Welcome to the Platform!

The Groundwater Accounting Platform has been designed to facilitate your data management. Much like a bank account, you will be able to view water usage and allocations as debits and credits to your water account. For each geography in which you own or manage parcels, you will see that geography's policies represented in the Platform. Such policies include decisions such as data types available to you and allocation amounts. You can find more information in the FAQs and in the Platform overview below.

Quick Links

Explore the Water Dashboard

Water Dashboard

The Water Dashboard is the central hub for all of your water data. View your Water Accounts, Parcels, and Wells.

A

Water Dashboard Navigation: Toggle between views of your Water Accounts, Parcels, and Wells.

B

Budget Data: Switch from the map view to a data-table view of your Water Account data. View, sort, and filter your data, or download the data for use in a spreadsheet.

C

Filter by Geography: When you have Water Accounts across multiple geographies, you can use this filter to narrow your view to a single geography.

D

List of Water Accounts: View a list of all of your Water Accounts. Click the “Details” link on any of the accounts to view the Water Budget.

E

Map of Water Accounts: View on map the outlines of all your Parcels and Water Accounts. Click on a shape to get a quick view of that Water Account’s details.

Water Budget

The Water Budget tab is the most up-to-date, at-a-glance view of your supply and usage data.

A

Display Options: Choose which year and period of data to display, as well as whether to show data in depth or volume.

B

Budget Overview: View the top-line numbers for “Total Usage”, “Total Supply”, and “Balance” for the current period.

C

Parcel Map: View on a map the parcel boundaries of the APNs contained in your Water Account.

D

Select a Water Account: Choose which of your Water Accounts to view.

E

Cumulative Water Usage Chart: Cumulative water use is displayed over time for the period. A red bar indicates the supply limit.

F

Monthly Water Usage Chart: Toggle to view a month-by-month view of water use in a bar chart.

G

Annual Usage Meter: The annual usage meter gives a quick overview of the period’s usage progress.

H

Supply: View a list of water supply sources and the amount given for each.

Account Activity

The Account Activity tab is your full ledger and history of water use debits and supply credits.

A

Display Options: Choose which year and period of data to display, as well as whether to show data in depth or volume.

B

Balance: View the top-line Balance value for the current period.

C

Last Transaction: View the latest transaction value for the current period.

D

All Transactions: Toggle to view a complete table of all transactions that can be exported as a CSV.

E

Recent Transactions: View a bank account-style transactions list of all recent usage and supply transactions for this Water Account for this period.

F

Transaction Subledger: Toggle the arrow on the right to view a parcel-by-parcel breakdown of usage or supply transactions for the month.

Parcel Detail

The Parcel Detail page provides water usage estimates for the parcel, in addition to other relevant data and information.

A

Water Account Link: Navigate directly to the Water Account that the Parcel belongs to.

B

Parcel Map: View the boundaries on the Parcel on a map.

C

Parcel Meta Data: View information about the Parcel’s area, zones, and ownership.

D

Water Measurements: View water use estimates for water measurements on the Parcel. The Platform can support metered data, remote telemetry data, and satellite-based evapotranspiration data.

E

Allocation Plan: For Geographies that have enabled Allocation Plans, this chart displays future water supplies into the future.
